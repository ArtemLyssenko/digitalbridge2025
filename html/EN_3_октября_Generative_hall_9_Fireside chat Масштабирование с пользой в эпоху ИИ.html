Business with a Human Face: AI, Data Ethics, and inDrive's Global Impact

Elmira Obri — Founder of Foody AI
Arsen Tomsky — Founder and Chief Executive Officer of inDrive

Annotation

They discussed a human-centered approach to AI and algorithmic transparency, criticized 'dark patterns' and monopolies. They examined how inDrive uses AI in 20+ areas—from personalization to safety—while maintaining fair pricing and low commissions. An impact strategy was presented through 'impact scalers' aiming to affect 1 billion people, along with an ecosystem of initiatives (EnVisionYou, SuperNova, Aurora). Special emphasis on supporting drivers (inDrive Money) and preparing for the era of driverless vehicles.

Goals

  • Ensure transparent and ethical AI deployment in inDrive's product and operational processes
  • Design and scale business models that reduce social injustice and abuse of power
  • Achieve impact on the lives of at least 1 billion people within 5 years through 'impact scalers'
  • Advance equal opportunities: social mobility, access to financing, and leadership development
  • Prepare for the industry's transition to autonomous transport and the retraining of drivers

Key Questions

  • How does inDrive implement AI in pricing, safety, operations, and HR, and how can algorithmic transparency be ensured?
  • How to prevent data manipulation and dark patterns by large platforms and preserve ethics?
  • How does the 'giving back' model work at inDrive and which 'impact scalers' are prioritized?
  • By what criteria should young leaders be selected and developed at EnVisionYou without harming self-respect?
  • How does inDrive Money reduce drivers' financial vulnerability, and what to do given the prospect of driverless vehicles?

Key Points

  • AI at inDrive is used in more than 20 areas: personalization of ride matching and enhanced safety (detecting fraud attempts).
  • Transparency and human-centeredness are key to trust; the company rejects price manipulation—prices are set by users.
  • Critique of 'squeezing the lemon' and dark patterns by monopolists; an example is a multi-click unsubscribe process used by competitors.
  • inDrive’s low commissions (≈9–12%) versus higher rates among competitors increase drivers’ earnings.
  • inDrive Money provides affordable loans where banks do not see drivers’ income, reducing dependence on MFIs.
  • Goal: within 5 years—measurable impact on 1 billion people through 'impact scalers' (IT, arts, media, government regulation, etc.).
  • Six target zones of injustice, including violence against women and children; systemic teams work to reduce these phenomena.
  • EnVisionYou is a university for leaders (not about children from rural areas); the ambition is to create 6 such universities in ~20 years; AI may help in talent selection.
  • Preparing for the future: as the shift to driverless vehicles happens, drivers are planned to be retrained and/or engaged as fleet owners.
  • An inDrive fund of $100 million has been created for investments and M&A; expansion is planned.

Conclusions

  • Human-centered ethics and algorithmic transparency are a competitive advantage against monopolistic practices.
  • Systemic impact through 'impact scalers' and an ecosystem of initiatives can scale the reduction of injustice globally.
  • Fintech solutions and low commissions strengthen drivers’ economic resilience before the era of driverless vehicles arrives.
  • Investments in leadership and education (EnVisionYou) create a long-term foundation for societal change.

Decisions Made

  • Preserve and protect the honest pricing model without algorithm manipulation.
  • Scale the use of AI in products and safety while adhering to transparency principles.
  • Advance toward the goal—1 billion beneficiaries in 5 years through the deployment of 'impact scalers'.
  • Expand inDrive Money and adjacent driver-support services into new markets.
  • Increase the activity of the investment fund ($100 million) and consider expanding it next year.
  • Launch a long-term program to create six EnVisionYou universities for leaders on a global scale.
