Key Points

  • AI and blockchain are complementary: the intelligence of the future will use the money of the future; agentic systems will require millions of microtransactions and a global on-chain infrastructure.
  • Tokenization will make illiquid assets (e.g., mining rights) monetizable and expand access to global liquidity, but it will require proper valuation and coordinated regulation.
  • BNB has evolved from an exchange token into a native asset of several blockchains (including BNB Greenfield), supporting a growing decentralized ecosystem and large projects such as PancakeSwap.
  • Kazakhstan is launching a stablecoin on BNB Chain and has built a comprehensive regulatory framework, forming a multi-stakeholder ecosystem and a 24/7 on-chain payments regime.
  • The historical four-year crypto market cycle points to a strong period through December; macro factors (rates, U.S. politics) may further support risk assets.
  • Decentralized markets will likely surpass centralized ones in scale and transparency; on-chain trading promotes price discovery.
  • Data protection and governance are critical for AI; blockchain can provide trust, verifiability, and a data-access economy.
  • Education and talent are the backbone of growth: Digital Academy trains tens of thousands of children worldwide, preparing talent for AI/web3.
  • The next 'big wave' is hard to predict; the focus is on quickly following trends and building useful products.
  • For entrepreneurs, what matters are the alignment of interest and competencies, a strong team, persistence, and a focus on long-term value.

Conclusions

  • The convergence of AI and blockchain will accelerate the shift to microtransactions and decentralized financial services with global accessibility.
  • Kazakhstan is solidifying itself as a regional web3 hub: mature regulation, the launch of a national stablecoin, and cooperation with global players.
  • Tokenization of real-world assets will expand financing and liquidity, but will require harmonized rules and pricing mechanisms.
  • Market conditions through year-end are likely favorable, however long-term success depends on practical use cases and product usefulness.
