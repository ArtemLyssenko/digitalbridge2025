Keynote speech: AI Platform — creation, training, and adaptation of an AI agent in 15 minutes for a person with no development experience

Рамиль Мухоряпов — Co-founder and CEO of Choco Holding, head of FreedomAI

Annotation

Рамиль Мухоряпов presented the Freedom AI strategy: free access to top models via Freedom GPT, a smart assistant with 49+ functions in the Freedom ecosystem, and the development of a benchmark Kazakh language model. The key focus is a platform that allows building AI agents without code in 10–15 minutes, already used inside 25 Freedom companies. The roadmap is to open the platform to external users at the beginning of next year and roll out “prompt‑to‑agent” and “prompt‑process.”

Goals

  • Democratize access to AI: provide citizens of Kazakhstan with free access to top models via Freedom GPT
  • Create a multi-functional assistant-friend that performs operations by voice/in chat with a generative UI and bank-grade security
  • Develop a benchmark Kazakh language model (KLM) to be independent from external providers
  • Build a platform that allows non‑developers to assemble AI agents in 10–15 minutes
  • Automate the business processes of Freedom companies and then open the platform to the market

Key Questions

  • How to ensure free and mass access to top GPT models without limits for citizens of Kazakhstan?
  • What features and trust measures does the assistant need to operate safely in banking and eGov scenarios?
  • How to cut AI agent creation from 30 days to 10–15 minutes and make it accessible to non‑tech specialists?
  • Why is an in‑house Kazakh language model needed and how will it outperform current external providers?
  • What is the roadmap for opening the platform to external users and expanding integrations (Telegram, Instagram, eGov)?

Key Points

  • Freedom GPT will give citizens of Kazakhstan free access to top models and lift usage limits
  • Freedom Assistant already supports 49+ functions: transfers in the bank, working with documents via eGov, purchases (including Freedom Mobile), doctor appointments, food delivery with a generative UI
  • The assistant is personalized: remembers preferences, plans and executes complex tasks with a single command (voice/text)
  • Development of a benchmark Kazakh language model (KLM) has been launched to avoid dependence on external providers; today the best model is by Yandex, which is “strange” for the local ecosystem
  • The Freedom AI platform allows you to build a bot in 10–15 minutes: choose from ~30 models, set temperature, prompt, quick knowledge base from files, playground
  • One‑click integrations via webhooks: Telegram already, Instagram requested by business teams; sub‑agents and logic linking are supported
  • Practical effect: the first agent took 30 days to build, now — 10–15 minutes; trained HR are able to automate processes on their own
  • Inside Freedom, 25 companies use the platform, >100 agents have been created, ~10,000 requests are processed per day
  • Roadmap: “prompt‑to‑agent” already works; the next step is “prompt‑process” to spin up entire processes with an army of interconnected agents
  • Public release of the platform is planned for the beginning of next year; Chocofood has been announced for rebranding to Freedom Дом

  • Freedom AI is building a full stack: from consumer services (GPT, assistant) to a platform for creating agents and a local Kazakh model
  • Creating and deploying AI agents is becoming a task for business specialists, not just developers, which accelerates digital transformation
  • Local language competence in Kazakh is a strategic priority for service quality and technological independence
  • The platform has proven its value on real use cases within Freedom and is preparing to scale to the external market

  • Continue internal scaling of the platform across all Freedom companies, then open the platform to external users at the beginning of next year

  • Develop “prompt‑to‑agent” and move to “prompt‑process” to automate entire business processes
