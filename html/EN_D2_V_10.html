Keynote speech: Overview of the TON Ecosystem, support for regional projects

Yugova Anastasia — Head of TON CIS Hub

Annotation

The session showed how the TON + Telegram bundle enables mass onboarding and distribution of Web3 products via Mini Apps. Cases (Catizen, Storm, eSIM) were presented with rapid growth and clear monetization. An exclusive TON and Telegram partnership starting 01.2025 and mandatory TON Connect integration were announced. A multi-level project support system was presented through hubs, TON Nest, marketing and grants, as well as regional initiatives and a partnership with Astana Hub.

Goals

  • Show the advantages of the TON ecosystem and distribution via Telegram for mass Web3 onboarding
  • Break down successful cases of launching and monetizing products inside TMA
  • Explain the new 2025 requirements: integration with TON and authentication via TON Connect
  • Present the support infrastructure: hubs, TON Nest, Builder School, marketing, grants
  • Engage regional teams and AI startups through meetups and a partnership with Astana Hub

Key Points

  • Telegram is a Web3-native messenger with a global reach of ~1 billion users; distribution inside the messenger is the key to scaling.
  • TON Wallet is natively available in Telegram Mini Apps; 130+ million users have already tried or use the wallet; sending crypto is as easy as sending a message.
  • Telegram Mini Apps open in one click without installation, which sharply reduces friction and user acquisition cost.
  • Starting January 2025, an exclusive partnership between TON and Telegram takes effect: all TMAs must have integration with the TON blockchain and authentication via TON Connect.
  • Catizen case: 10 million users in 2 months; a successful airdrop with correct tokenomics and in-app purchases; further scaling of the gaming ecosystem.
  • Storm case (trading): a product adapted for TON generates ~$80K in monthly revenue.
  • eSIM case: a utilitarian eSIM purchase inside TMA with payment in TON/USDT — a clear user pain point and transparent monetization.
  • Low CAC is achieved thanks to the existing user base in Telegram and content strategy in channels; retention is supported through promos, features, and direct communications.
  • There are 700+ active teams in the ecosystem; support is built as a “pyramid”: hubs as an entry point, TON Nest, Builder School, tech/product support, marketing and listing in the Telegram Apps Center, and at the scaling stage — grants and fundraising.
  • Regional activity: online reviews and offline meetups across the CIS; the partnership of TON CIS Hub with Astana Hub (AIpreneurs) is confirmed; a builders’ meetup at Astana Hub has been announced.
