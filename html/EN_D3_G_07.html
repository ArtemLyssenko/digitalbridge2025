Decentrathon and Robotics Competitions Award Ceremony — Digital Bridge

Arsen Tomsky — Founder and Chief Executive Officer of inDrive

Annotation

The ceremony summed up Decentrathon and the Robotics Competitions with the participation of key industry partners. Winners were announced in tracks from inDrive, BCC iHUB, and Solana (Best Web3 Solution), as well as in the FIRST Tech Challenge and the Robotics Hackathon. Partners highlighted the scale of the event, the growth of AI/Web3 ecosystems, and a leap forward in school and student robotics in Kazakhstan. Plans were announced to scale the championships and integrate the best solutions into business and blockchain ecosystems.

Goals

  • Recognize team achievements and present awards for the Decentrathon and Robotics tracks
  • Strengthen collaboration among business, government, and startups for the implementation of AI/Web3 solutions
  • Highlight the scale and prospects of school and student robotics in the country
  • Launch further advancement of the best projects into the Solana ecosystem and corporate partners

Key Questions

  • How to take hackathon prototypes to pilots and implementation with industrial partners?
  • What financing and mentorship mechanisms will ensure the growth of the AI/Web3 ecosystem in Kazakhstan?
  • How to build a continuous pathway from school robotics to university competitions and industry?
  • What metrics should be used to assess the impact of championships and acceleration on the economy and society?

Key Points

  • inDrive: winners — team Sapiens (first track) and team «Ельорна» (second track).
  • BCC iHUB (AI tracks): winners — team «208» (first track) and team «Star Wars» (second track).
  • Solana Best Web3 Solution: winners — «Солариум» (first track) and «Нео Дэф» (second track); the high quality of applications and the interest of Solana Foundation were noted.
  • FIRST Tech Challenge: awarded «Exlands» (Finalist Alliance Partner), «PID & Kiroshi» (Finalist Alliance Captain), «Red Lotus» (Winning Alliance Partner), and «Желто Сан» (a separate nomination).
  • Robotics Hackathon: 3rd place — «Максат и Ко», 2nd place — «Дудакшн», 1st place — «Кинологи».
  • BCC iHUB set tasks on personalized offers for customers and advanced AI-based text analysis.
  • Solana Day by Decentrathon — the first large-scale Solana event of this level in Kazakhstan with the support of Blockchain Center, Astana Hub, and МЦРИАП.
  • In robotics: 20,000 schoolchildren across the country, plan — to hold 150 championships in the season; increase in quotas for the world championship in Houston from 2 to 7.
  • The national team of RK is preparing for the world championship in Panama; previously — three years without defeat (Singapore, Athens, Geneva).
  • Announcement of new student leagues: launch of Robocon and RoboCup in Kazakhstan with finals in Hong Kong.
